Hi, I am a PhD student at the Department of Pure and Applied Mathematics, [Waseda University](https://www.waseda.jp/top/en/), Japan, supervised by [Dr. Momoko Hayamizu](https://researchmap.jp/momoko_hayamizu?lang=en). I am also a JSPS Research Fellow funded by the [Japan Society for the Promotion of Science](https://www.jsps.go.jp/english/).

Before joining Waseda University, I received my Master's degree from [National Chengchi University](https://www.nccu.edu.tw/index.php?Lang=en), Taiwan, under the supervision of [Prof. Michael Fuchs](https://web.math.nccu.edu.tw/mfuchs/), and my Bachelor's degree from [Lanzhou University](https://global.lzu.edu.cn/), China.

## Research Interests

- Combinatorial phylogenetics
- Analytic and enumerative combinatorics
- Computational biology
